Chocolate Avocado Pudding

Saw this at the market the other day. The ingredients were simple and it looked delicious! Many substitutes abound (liquid, sweetener, spices). Vary amounts to taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 ½ ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened

Steps

  1. Heat cream in a small saucepan until small bubbles appear around edges.
  2. Combine avocado and cream cheese in a food processor and blend until smooth.
  3. Add lime juice and process until smooth; whisk into the cooled cream mixture.
  4. Fill an ice cream maker with the avocado mixture, and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions for making ice cream.
