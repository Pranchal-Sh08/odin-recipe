The Ultimate Berry Crumble

I was only half kidding about the “ultimate” designation for this, as it truly was everything I’ve ever wanted in a fresh berry crumble.
This has the perfect balance of sweet and tart, tender and crisp, not to mention copious amounts of butter in our double application of crumble.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 1 cup blackberries

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
